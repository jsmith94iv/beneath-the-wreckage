About

Beneath the Wreckage was forged in the industrial underbelly of Baltimore, where steel meets sorrow and every song is a reckoning. We weld heavy alt-rock riffs to post-grunge melancholy, layering double-kick drums, guttural screams, and angelic choruses into a sound that’s as brutal as it is beautiful. Our music dives deep—into deception, betrayal, heartbreak, defiance, urgency, and introspection. These aren’t just themes; they’re battle scars. Each track is a cathartic release, built for cracked concrete, 3 a.m. drives, and the moments you face yourself in the dark. Together we stand: Broken. Loud. Beautiful.
